Chaufa Rice

Description

Chaufa Rice is one of the most popular Peruvian plates mainly because of it's simplicity.

Personally, this is one of my go-to meals in College as it is quick and delicious. The leftovers are usually even more tasty!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Green Onion
  • Chicken
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in your preferred way and reserve
  2. Cook chicken in small cubes and reserve
  3. Add 3 eggs to a pan and mix
  4. Add the rice, soy sauce, and mix
  5. Add the chicken and chopped green onions
  6. Mix and serve, reserve any leftovers
