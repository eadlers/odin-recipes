Lomo Saltado

Description

Probably my favorite Peruvian dish, this asian/peruvian fusion plate will form an explosion of flavors in your mouth!

A more complicated and expensive recipe but totally worth it

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Steak
  • Fries
  • Red Onions
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • White vinegar
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook rice in your preferred way and reserve
  2. Cook steak in cubes and reserve
  3. Cook Fries in your preferred way
  4. Mix oyster sauce, soy sauce, and white vinegar
  5. Add cooked steak, onions and tomatoes into pan with the sauce in medium heat and wait until it boils
  6. Serve with rice and fries
  7. Add any leftover juice to rice for extra flavor
